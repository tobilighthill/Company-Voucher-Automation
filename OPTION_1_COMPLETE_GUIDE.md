## üîç Troubleshooting

### "The approver didn't get the confirmation email"
**Solution**: Check SPAM/Junk folder. Search for "FormSubmit" or "noreply@formsubmit.co"

### "They clicked confirm but still no voucher"
**Solution**: Wait 2-3 minutes, then check spam again. If still nothing, send another test voucher.

### "The new tab shows an error"
**Solution**: This usually means the email format was wrong. Check for typos in the email address.

### "I want to test it myself first"
**Solution**: 
1. Enter YOUR OWN email in "Approved By"
2. Send a test voucher
3. Check your inbox/spam for the confirmation
4. Click confirm
5. You'll receive the voucher
6. Now you know exactly what the approver will experience!
